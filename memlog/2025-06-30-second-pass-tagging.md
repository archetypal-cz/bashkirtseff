4. **Minor characters** - One-time mentions not consistently tagged

### Tagging Best Practices Reinforced
- Tag ALL significant mentions, not just first appearance
- Include family relationship terms (ma tante, papa, grand-papa)
- Tag entities in dialogue when they're the subject of discussion
- Create glossary entries for any repeatedly mentioned person/place
