## Summary

Carnet 047 of Marie Bashkirtseff's diary. An eleven-day period dominated by the return of Audiffret to Nice, Marie's oscillating emotions, the invention of the "Bibi" nickname system, and the establishment of a private code language. Features Marie's anagram session, the Charlotte de Montmorency balcony scene, the Alice de Nevers theater premiere with Audiffret visiting her box, the Cima-Marie Sapogenikoff abandonment subplot, extended proto-feminist meditation on gender dynamics, and the creation of the "couronne du martyr."

**Date range**: 1875-10-13 to 1875-10-23
**Entry count**: 11
**Location(s)**: Nice
**Paragraph range**: 047.0667 - 047.1106

## Key Themes

- **"Bibi" system**: Originally Lord Loftus's nickname, becomes universal term applied to everyone; Marie writes extended passages in third person as "Bibi"
- **Anagrams**: "Emile Audiffret" = "Fou frit d'Amelie"; "L'Andriot Saetone" = "Triton dans l'eau"; etc.
- **Neologism verbs**: "terffiduer," "enoteasser," "pepiner," "hamiltonner" — invented words replacing "aimer"
- **Gioia returns**: Encounters on the Promenade; complex love-hate ("je l'aime tout en la voulant etrangler")
- **Audiffret returns**: Windows open Oct 17; first encounters at Promenade and Alice de Nevers premiere
- **Charlotte de Montmorency balcony scene**: Marie stages candlelit display from pavillon balcony
- **Alice de Nevers premiere**: Audiffret visits Marie's theater box; tense controlled conversation
- **Cima abandonment**: Marie Sapogenikoff's beau stops writing; Marie advises her to break it off with dignity
- **Proto-feminist meditation**: Extended analysis of men's tactical behavior in courtship (Oct 21)
- **"Couronne du martyr"**: Marie creates physical crown of thorns from rose branches
- **Final resolve**: "Je ne veux plus aimer cet homme" (Oct 23)
