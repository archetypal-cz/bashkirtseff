# Comtesse de Galve

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-22

## Identity
Countess who has moved to London, envied by Marie.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 22**: Marie envies her: "La bienheureuse comtesse de Galve vient de s'installer à Londres, l'heureuse femme. Vivre parmi les Anglais de la plus haute société, c'est le comble du bonheur" (The blessed Countess de Galve has just settled in London, the happy woman. To live among the English of the highest society is the height of happiness).

## Context
- Recently moved to London
- Access to highest English society
- Marie considers her "blessed" and "happy"
- Represents escape from Nice
- Possibly related to "papa Galve"
