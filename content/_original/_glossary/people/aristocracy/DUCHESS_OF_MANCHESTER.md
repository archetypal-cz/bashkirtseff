## Basic Information

%% GLO_DUCHESS_OF_MANCHESTER.0002 %%
The Duchess of Manchester was Countess Luise Friederike Auguste von Alten (15 October 1832 -- 15 July 1911), a German noblewoman who married William Montagu, 7th Duke of Manchester, in 1852. She was the mother of Lady Mary Montagu, who married the Duke of Hamilton in 1873.

## Connection to Marie's Diary

%% GLO_DUCHESS_OF_MANCHESTER.0004 %%
As the mother-in-law of the Duke of Hamilton -- the man Marie obsessed over -- the Duchess of Manchester occupies a place in the aristocratic network Marie desperately wished to penetrate. She was known as a prominent society hostess and one of the leaders of London's social scene.
