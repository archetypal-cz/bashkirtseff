## Basic Information

%% GLO_DUCHESSE_DE_MONTMORENCY.0002 %%
The Montmorency family was one of the oldest and most prestigious noble families of France, tracing their lineage to the medieval period. The title "First Baron of France" was associated with the Montmorency name. A Duchesse de Montmorency in Marie's era would have represented the pinnacle of the old French aristocracy.

## Connection to Marie's Diary

%% GLO_DUCHESSE_DE_MONTMORENCY.0004 %%
The Montmorency name appears in Marie's diary as part of the aristocratic social world she navigated. The family name itself carried enormous prestige in French society, and encounters with such figures reinforced Marie's awareness of the social hierarchies she both coveted and resented.
