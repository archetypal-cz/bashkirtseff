# Grand Duke Vladimir (Voldemar)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-30

## Identity
Russian Grand Duke Vladimir Alexandrovich (1847-1909), son of Emperor Alexander II. Marie remembers him as exemplifying aristocratic perfection like the Hamiltons.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 30**: "Je me souviens vaguement des grands-ducs Voldemar et Alexis, mais je me souviens assez pour savoir que ce sont deux hommes dans le genre Hamilton." Their walk, eyes, gestures "tout est grand et sublime, incomparable."

## Historical Context
- Son of Alexander II
- Brother of future Alexander III
- Known for cultural patronage
- Married Marie of Mecklenburg-Schwerin 1874
- President of Imperial Academy of Arts

## Context
- Met in Nice or Baden-Baden
- Exemplifies aristocratic ideal
- Compared to Hamilton brothers
- "Hommes comme j'aime"
- Grand bearing and sublime manner
