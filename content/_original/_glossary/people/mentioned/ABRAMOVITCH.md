# Abramovitch

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-21

## Identity
Previous visitor to the Bashkirtseff household, likely Russian based on patronymic name.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 21**: Marie compares Count Markoff's unexpected visit to "la visite d'adieu du pauvre Abramovitch" (poor Abramovitch's farewell visit). The comparison makes her uncomfortable about receiving Markoff alone.

## Context
- "Pauvre" (poor) suggests either death or departure
- Farewell visit implies he left Nice or died
- Used as reference point for inappropriate male visits
- Shows social protocols about receiving men alone
