# Barter Family

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
English family in Nice society. Marie considers them ordinary, not part of the elegant world.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At Howard dance: "il n'y a que des Tebbitt, des Barter et dans ce genre" - only the Tebbitts, the Barters and that type. Marie finds them inadequate for a grand social event.

## Context
- Part of English colony in Nice
- Not considered "monde élégant" by Marie
- Grouped with Tebbitt family as ordinary English
- Representative of middle-class English society

## Research Notes
- Typical of English families wintering in Nice
- Marie's snobbery shows in her dismissal of such families
