# Chevalier

## Overview

A young man in Marie's Nice social circle during summer 1875. Identified as "secrétaire de la préfecture" (secretary of the prefecture) in carnet 036.

## Role in Marie's Life

- Part of the group of young men who attend social gatherings at Marie's home
- Dances with Marie at the bal champêtre (July 11, 1875)
- Initiates the "fossette" (dimple) parlor game during an evening at Marie's house (July 13, 1875)

## Research Notes

- First name unknown; position as prefecture secretary suggests minor civil servant status
- Appears alongside Audiffret, Saetone, Godard in Marie's social circle
