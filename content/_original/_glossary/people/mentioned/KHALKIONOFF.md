# Khalkionoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-14

## Identity
A young Russian man who dies in Nice on March 14, 1874 at 5 PM. Marie remembers him as good, upright, and modest.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 14**: Dies at 5 PM: "Pauvre Khalkionoff, aujourd'hui à cinq heures il a cessé de souffrir." Marie calls him "un si bon garçon, si bon, si droit, si modeste" (such a good boy, so good, so upright, so modest). His mother had come to Nice. Previously saved by Dr. Walitsky from similar attack, but mother chose Dr. Rehberg this time.
- **March 16**: Marie's mother attends his funeral. Marie originally wrote "bienheureux" (blessed) then crossed it out before Khalkionoff's name.

## Context
- Young Russian man (called "garçon")
- Died from recurring medical condition
- Mother present for final illness
- Medical controversy: Walitsky had saved him before but mother switched to Rehberg
- Marie feels Walitsky treated more impolitely
- Marie accepts death as God's will
