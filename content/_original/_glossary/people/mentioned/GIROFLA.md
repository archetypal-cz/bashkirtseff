# Girofla

## Overview

"Girofla" is Marie Bashkirtseff's nickname for Emile d'Audiffret, a young Nicois nobleman. The name comes from the operetta Girofle-Girofla by Charles Lecocq (1874), where Girofla is one of the twin brides.

See main entry: [AUDIFFRET](../recurring/AUDIFFRET.md)

## Usage in Diary

Marie and her family use "Girofla" as a code name for Audiffret throughout 1875, particularly when discussing him in letters and conversation. In carnet 039, Marie's aunt writes (in Russian, translated to French by Marie) about "Girofla" visiting Nice and possibly coming to Wiesbaden.
