# Machenka

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-28

## Overview

Wife of Uncle Stiopa who visits Nice with him in January 1874 and continues as regular household companion.

## Relationship to Marie

Aunt by marriage - wife of Marie's Uncle Stiopa (Stepan).

## Characteristics

- Marie criticizes her taste, calling her shopping choices "mauvais genre" (bad style)
- Marie says she cannot get used to her manners
- Despite criticism, becomes regular companion on outings

## Social Activities

- Visits Nice with husband Stiopa
- Brings Russian salted foods and preserves
- Stays with Walitsky during visits
- Shops for hats at Mortier

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 7**: Shopping trip criticized by Marie
- **March 28**: Accompanies family to Monaco casino, walks with Marie to casino, returns early on 5 o'clock train

### 1875
- **August 16**: Accompanies Marie and Dina on a trip to Wiesbaden to find a carriage and horses, encounters German children shouting "hourra!" at them, moonlit carriage ride home discussing elopement and marriage
