# Catherine Eristoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-01-24
**Diary Coverage**: Up to July 1881

Catherine is Nini's sister (Nini being married to Paul, Marie's cousin). She is identified as "sœur de Nini" and accompanies the family during their summer at Gavronzi.

**Key Diary References**:
- July 9, 1881: Listed among those traveling to Kremontchougy: "M. et Mme, Dina, la princesse, Catherine, sœur de Nini et Sperandio"

**Context**:
- Sister of Nini (Paul's wife)
- Part of the extended family circle at Gavronzi
- Travels with the family on excursions
- Member of the Eristoff family (Georgian princely family)
