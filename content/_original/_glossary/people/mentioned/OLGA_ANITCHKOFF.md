# Olga Anitchkoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-19

## Identity
Member of the Anitchkoff family, known for erratic social behavior that Marie compares to her mother's.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 19**: Marie's mother has "sorties comme Olga Anitchkoff" (outbursts like Olga Anitchkoff) - leaves abruptly without explanation. Marie describes this behavior as "comme une enragée, comme une folle" (like someone enraged, like a madwoman).
