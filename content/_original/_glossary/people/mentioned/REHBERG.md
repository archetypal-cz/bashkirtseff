# Dr. Rehberg

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-14

## Identity
Doctor in Nice chosen by Khalkionoff's mother to replace Dr. Walitsky. Marie implies he provided less care than Walitsky.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 14**: Khalkionoff's mother "voulut Rehberg" and acted "assez impoliment" with Walitsky. Marie notes Walitsky "y apportait beaucoup plus de soins que Rehberg" (brought much more care than Rehberg). Patient dies under Rehberg's care.
