## Identity
Russian couple (M. et Mme Filimonoff) who request to hear Marie sing at the Howard dance.

- **April 20**: Request to hear Marie sing after she has already performed for the Howards, Bosslovleffs, and Nadia. Marie sings in a dark room, terrified but gives satisfactory performance.
