# Louis XIV (The Sun King)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-16

## Historical Figure
King of France (1638-1715), reigned 1643-1715. Known for absolute monarchy, Versailles, and numerous mistresses.

## Marie's Fascination

### 1874
- **January 16**: "Je rougis au nom de Louis XIV" (I blush at the name of Louis XIV)
- "Il était un bon garçon" (He was a good fellow)
- "Si je vivais en même temps avec lui je l'adorerais" (If I lived at the same time as him I would adore him)

## Marie's Perception
- Sees him as romantic figure, not just historical monarch
- Attracted to his power and glamour
- Calls herself "strange" for blushing at his name
- Projects onto him as ideal of masculine power and gallantry

## Cultural Context
- Louis XIV as romantic hero in 19th century literature
- Symbol of absolute power and refined culture
- His court represented height of aristocratic splendor
- His mistresses achieved quasi-official status

## Significance for Marie
- Represents everything she yearns for: power, beauty, court life
- Her crush reveals romantic nature and historical imagination
- Shows her attraction to powerful men who appreciate women
- Part of her fantasy life about belonging to aristocratic circles
