# Princesse Kotchoubey

## Overview

Member of the prominent Kochubey (Kotchoubey) Russian aristocratic family. Marie encounters her at the skating rink at the Cercle de la Méditerranée in Nice.

## Historical Context

The Kochubey family was one of the most distinguished families of the Russian Empire, descended from Ukrainian Cossack nobility. Several princes Kochubey held high positions in Russian government.

## Relevance to Marie

Marie shows herself off to the princess at the skating rink. The princess is the sister-in-law of a prince Marie met during her time in Russia. Her husband recognized and greeted Maman at church, having known her when she was Marie's age.
