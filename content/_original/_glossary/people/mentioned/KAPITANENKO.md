# Kapitanenko

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-01-24
**Diary Coverage**: Up to July 1881

Kapitanenko is mentioned as someone who was fetched to attend Paul and Nini's wedding celebration at Micha's country estate.

**Key Diary References**:
- July 13, 1881: "on a soupé jusqu'à sept heures du matin en compagnie du prêtre, de sa femme, d'une autre dame et de Kapitanenko qu'on est allé chercher"

**Context**:
- Part of the local social circle in the Poltava region
- Invited to Paul and Nini's wedding supper which lasted until 7 AM
- Important enough to be specifically fetched for the occasion
- The wedding was held at Micha's country estate
