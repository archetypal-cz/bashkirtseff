# Mary Sapogenikoff

## Basic Information

Mary Sapogenikoff was the daughter of Nina Sapogenikoff, part of a Russian family who were close friends of the Bashkirtseffs in Nice.

## Connection to Marie

Mary, along with her sister Olga, was one of Marie's closest friends in Nice. Together they formed the society of the "Three Graces" in 1875. The Sapogenikoff family was part of the Russian expatriate community in Nice and maintained close relations with the Bashkirtseff household.
