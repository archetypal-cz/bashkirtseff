# Pietro Antonelli

## Basic Information

Count Pietro Antonelli was Marie's "suitor" in Rome during 1876. He was the nephew of Cardinal Giacomo Antonelli (1806-1876), Vatican Secretary of State under Pope Pius IX.

## Family

- **Father**: Count Luigi (Louis) Antonelli
- **Mother**: Countess Camilla Antonelli, née Folchi
- **Brothers**: Domenico (nicknamed "Savage") and Paolo (nicknamed "Lion")
- **Uncle**: Cardinal Giacomo Antonelli, one of the most powerful figures in the Vatican

## Nicknames in Marie's Diary

Marie gave him multiple nicknames: *Pazzerello* (little crazy one), *Monk*, *Cardinalino* (little cardinal), *Pietruccio*.

## Relationship with Marie

Marie met Pietro at a masquerade in Rome in 1876. Discussion of marriage between Marie and Pietro followed their meeting. Key events:
- Marie and her mother visited Naples where Marie also met Count Alexander de Larderel
- Back in Nice, Marie returned to Rome with her aunt, hoping Antonelli would reassure her
- In Rome, she met Antonelli again but remained unconvinced of his devotion
- On their last night together, Antonelli asked her to marry him; Marie allowed him to kiss her and joined in the kiss (which she later remembered with shame)
- Marie and Pietro never saw each other again after this Roman visit
