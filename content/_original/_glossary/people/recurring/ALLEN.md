# The Allen Family

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-22

## Identity
English or American family in Nice including Mr. Allen, Mrs. Allen, and their son "le jeune Allen," who socialize with the Bashkirtseff and Howard families.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 10**: Mrs. Allen is "très, très aimable" to Marie at the Howard children's theater performance
- **February 15**: Allen present at the Howards after Filimonoff wedding visit
- **February 22**: Marie visits Mrs. Allen with her mother, finding Mrs. Howard there. The men (Mr. Allen, young Allen, and Tebbitt) sit on one side, women on the other.

## Context
- English-speaking family in Nice
- Close with the Howard family
- Formal visiting relationships maintained
- Young Allen appears to be similar age to Marie and other young people
- Traditional gender separation during visits
- Part of the respectable international community
