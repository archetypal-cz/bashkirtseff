# Derwies

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-25

## Identity
A person (likely wealthy) who hosts concerts in Nice that Marie is eager to attend.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 25**: Marie hopes Dina won't learn of her brother's death until after "le concert chez Derwies" (the concert at Derwies'). Marie expresses strong desire to attend: "J'ai tant envie d'y aller."

## Context
- Hosts private concerts in Nice
- Part of the cultural social scene
- Events significant enough that Marie doesn't want them disrupted by bad news
- Name suggests possible German or Baltic German origin
- Wealthy enough to host musical events
