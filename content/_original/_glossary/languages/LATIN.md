# Latin

- **Mock-formality**: Playful pseudo-scholarly tone
- **Classical education**: Demonstrating her learning
- **Traditional expressions**: Common Latin phrases of the era

- "propria persona" - in person
- "nec plus ultra" - the ultimate, nothing beyond
- "ad infinitum" - to infinity
- Standard Latin expressions common in 19th-century educated speech
