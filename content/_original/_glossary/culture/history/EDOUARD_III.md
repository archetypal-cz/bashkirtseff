## Overview

%% GLO_EDOUARD_III.0002 %%
Edward III (1312-1377) was King of England from 1327 to 1377. His reign saw the beginning of the Hundred Years' War with France, the devastating Black Death of 1348-1350, and the founding of the Order of the Garter. He is remembered as one of the most successful medieval English monarchs, transforming England into a major military power.
