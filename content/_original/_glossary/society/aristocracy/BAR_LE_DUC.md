# Bar-le-Duc

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-23

## Identity
Race horse from Duke of Hamilton's stable, ridden to multiple victories by Prince Louis Esterhazy.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 23**: "Avec Bar-le-Duc, l'un des chevaux de l'écurie du duc de Hamilton, il a remporté l'année dernière, en steeple chase, plusieurs victoires importantes" (With Bar-le-Duc, one of the horses from the Duke of Hamilton's stable, he won several important steeplechases last year).

## Context
- Named after French city Bar-le-Duc
- Part of Hamilton's racing stable
- Successful steeplechaser
- Ridden by Prince Esterhazy
- Won "plusieurs victoires importantes"
- Symbol of Hamilton's sporting interests
