# Mme Randouin

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-18

## Identity
Woman in Nice society with whom someone (likely Hamilton) danced.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 18**: While looking at race programs, Marie finds "his name" and remembers "la journée grise et pluvieuse où il a dansé une ronde avec Mme Randouin etc." (the grey and rainy day when he danced a round dance with Mme Randouin etc.)

## Significance
- Part of Marie's obsessive memory collection about Hamilton
- The "round dance" memory from a previous social event
- Shows Marie's detailed tracking of Hamilton's social interactions
- The "etc." suggests there were other dance partners that day
