# Les demoiselles Roslowleff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-14-15

## Identity
Young Russian aristocratic women, part of Princess Souvoroff's social circle in Nice.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 14**: Present in Princess Souvoroff's rented tribune at the Cercle de la Méditerranée concert, contributing to the brilliant social gathering that emphasizes Marie's isolation.

## Context
- Russian aristocratic family name
- Young unmarried women of high society
- Part of Princess Souvoroff's exclusive circle
- Represent the kind of social acceptance Marie desperately desires
- Their presence in the tribune highlights Marie's exclusion from aristocratic society
- Associated with other Russian nobles and European aristocrats
