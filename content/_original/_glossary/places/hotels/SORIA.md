# Soria

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-16

## Identity
A handsome young man whom Marie's mother considers a more suitable romantic interest than Comte Lambertye.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 16**: Marie's mother praises his beauty and suggests Marie should fall in love with someone like Soria instead of Lambertye - "on peut ainsi penser ou chanter une romance" (one can think or sing a romance). Marie admits she prefers even Lambertye to Soria.

## Context
- Considered handsome by Marie's mother
- Represents "appropriate" romantic interest for young Marie
- Contrasted with Lambertye who mother sees as unsuitable
- Spanish or Italian name suggesting Mediterranean nobility
- Mother suggests he would inspire innocent romantic feelings
- Marie rejects him in favor of her complex obsession with Lambertye
