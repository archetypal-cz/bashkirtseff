# Hotel de Luxembourg

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-09
**Diary Coverage**: Up to April 5, 1875

Luxury hotel in Nice where the Prince of Wales stayed during his visit in March-April 1875. Marie addresses her anonymous letter requesting a portrait to "A Son Altesse Royale, Monseigneur le Prince de Galles, Hôtel de Luxembourg à Nice."

## Key Events in Diary

- **April 4, 1875**: Marie writes anonymous letter to Prince of Wales at this address
- **April 5, 1875**: In her dream, she imagines the letter being delivered here

## Historical Context

High-end accommodation suitable for visiting royalty and aristocracy in 19th-century Nice. The Prince of Wales was known for his travels to the French Riviera.
