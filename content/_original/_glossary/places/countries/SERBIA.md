- **Congress of Berlin (1878)**: Serbia gained full independence
- **Kingdom (1882)**: Serbia proclaimed a kingdom under King Milan I
- **Pan-Slavism**: Serbia was a focus of Pan-Slavic sentiment, particularly in Russia
