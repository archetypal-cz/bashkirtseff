# Te Deum

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-23

## Definition
Ancient Latin Christian hymn of praise, traditionally sung at special celebrations and thanksgivings.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 23**: Sung at Russian Orthodox church in Nice to celebrate the marriage of Grand Duchess Marie Alexandrovna to Prince Alfred, Duke of Edinburgh.

## Context
- "Te Deum laudamus" (We praise thee, O God)
- Used for royal occasions, military victories, celebrations
- Part of both Catholic and Orthodox traditions
- Shows Russian community maintaining religious observances abroad
