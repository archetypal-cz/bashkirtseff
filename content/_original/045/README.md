## Summary

Carnet 045 of Marie Bashkirtseff's diary. A dramatic week dominated by Marie's rivalry with the courtesan Gioia over Emile d'Audiffret ("Girofla"). Features elaborate disguise pranks, proto-feminist reflections on sexual double standards, anonymous threatening letters, and Marie's remarkable psychological self-analysis.

**Date range**: 1875-09-26 to 1875-10-02
**Entry count**: 7
**Location(s)**: Nice
**Paragraph range**: 045.0001 - 045.0353

## Key Themes

- **Audiffret/Gioia affair**: Audiffret abandons Marie for the courtesan Gioia
- **Disguise pranks**: Marie dresses as old man twice (Sep 26, Sep 29)
- **Le Proces Veauradieux motif**: Running joke about "bonnet grec et robe de chambre"
- **Anonymous letters**: Three threatening letters sent to Audiffret and his father
- **Sexual double standards**: Extended philosophical passage (Sep 28) on men's freedom vs women's constraint
- **Self-analysis**: Marie distinguishes "surface" emotions from her "fond" (core self)
