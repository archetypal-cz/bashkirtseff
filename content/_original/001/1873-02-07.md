%% 001.0106 %%
%% [#Nice](../_glossary/places/cities/NICE.md) [#Duke_of_Hamilton](../_glossary/people/core/DUKE_OF_HAMILTON.md) [#Gioia](../_glossary/places/cities/GIOIA.md) %%
