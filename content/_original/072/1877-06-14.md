The marriage-as-fortress proverb ("ceux qui sont dehors veulent y entrer, ceux qui sont dedans veulent en sortir") is commonly misattributed to the Quran; it actually derives from Montaigne (Essais III.5), who attributed it to various sources. Marie attributes it to the Quran. The bee invasion she takes as an omen of wealth. %%
Depuis deux jours nos chambres sont envahies par des abeilles. On dit que cela porte bonheur ou richesse.

%% 072.0045 %%
%% 2026-02-02T12:30:00 LAN: Proverb attributed to the Quran by Marie - actually a French saying %%
