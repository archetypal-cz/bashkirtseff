# Lundi, 3 février 1879

%% 084.0098 %%
%% [#Paris](../_glossary/places/cities/PARIS.md) [#Paul_de_Cassagnac](../_glossary/people/mentioned/PAUL_DE_CASSAGNAC.md) [#Dr_Lannelongue](../_glossary/people/mentioned/DR_LANNELONGUE.md) [#Coube](../_glossary/people/mentioned/COUBE.md) [#Menton](../_glossary/places/cities/MENTON.md) [#Mme_Abaza](../_glossary/people/aristocracy/MME_ABAZA.md) [#San_Remo](../_glossary/places/cities/SAN_REMO.md) [#Dourassoff](../_glossary/people/mentioned/DOURASSOFF.md) [#Bihovetz](../_glossary/people/mentioned/BIHOVETZ.md) [#Maman](../_glossary/people/family/MAMAN.md) %%
%% 2026-02-02T14:30:00 LAN: "fichu" - colloquial: done for, finished, ruined %%
%% 2026-02-02T14:30:00 LAN: "voix de majorité" - margin of victory in votes %%
%% 2026-02-09T18:00:00 RSR: Condom election results: Cassagnac wins but with drastically reduced majority - only 990 votes versus 4,131 in the 1877 elections. Marie hopes "next time he'll be completely finished." The Condom by-election of February 3, 1879 took place in the aftermath of the republican victory in the January 5 senatorial elections that led to MacMahon's resignation and Grevy's election as President. Mother publicizes Marie's medal to the Russian expatriate network along the Riviera: Coube family in Menton, Mme Abaza in San Remo, Dourassoff and Bihovetz also present. %%
Cassagnac est élu mais il n'a que 990 voix de majorité, aux dernières élections en 1877 il avait 4.131 voix de majorité. Ce qui fait espérer qu'à la prochaine fois il sera fichu tout à fait.

%% 2026-02-02T14:30:00 LAN: "ma médaille" - refers to her Julian Academy award/medal for painting %%
%% 2026-02-02T14:30:00 LAN: SPELLING VARIANT: "féliciations" - should be "félicitations" %%
Maman a bien entendu fait part de ma médaille à tout le monde. Les Coubé qui sont à Menton m'envoient un télégramme de féliciations. Mme Abaza est à San Remo, tout ce monde se voit. Dourassoff est aussi là, et Bihovetz.
