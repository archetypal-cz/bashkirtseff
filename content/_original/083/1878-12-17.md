# Mardi, 17 decembre 1878

%% 083.1089 %%
%% [#Paris](../_glossary/places/cities/PARIS.md) [#Paul](../_glossary/people/core/PAUL.md) %%
%% 2026-02-09T14:34:00 RSR: Marie reaches her emotional nadir: "I have rarely been so morose and so lost." Paul "behaves well" - she even finds him "too proper," suggesting his reformed conduct robs her of dramatic friction. %%
