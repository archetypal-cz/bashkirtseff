# Duke of Buckingham

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-03

## Identity
George Villiers, 1st Duke of Buckingham (1592-1628), English courtier and favorite of King James I and Charles I.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 3**: Marie reading about him in "Louis XIV et son siècle," becomes obsessed. "Il n'y a qu'un Anglais qui peut être aussi beau." Notes he was "aussi gros que Hamilton" (as fat as Hamilton). "Hamilton est un Buckingham moderne!"

## Historical Context
- Famous for beauty and charm
- Romantic figure in French history (affair with Anne of Austria)
- Assassinated in 1628
- Marie's ideal of aristocratic masculinity
- Connects to her Hamilton obsession
