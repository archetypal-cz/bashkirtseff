# Galula

**Research Status**: Moderate
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-10
**Diary Coverage**: Up to May 28, 1875

A young man in Nice society who becomes the crucial intermediary between Marie Bashkirtseff and Emile Audiffret. He plays a key role in facilitating their first formal introduction.

## Key Role in Marie's Story

- **Friend of Audiffret**: Has close relationship with Emile Audiffret
- **Intermediary**: Brings Audiffret to meet Marie at the beach on May 28, 1875
- **Social Facilitator**: Helps arrange the formal introduction between Marie and Audiffret
- **Charming Personality**: Marie describes him as "charmant garçon" who speaks very nicely

## Key Events

- **May 28, 1875**: Facilitates the historic first meeting between Marie and Audiffret
- Helps Marie down from carriage, brings her a bench
- Sits on the ground initially, then finds his own seat
- Brings Audiffret over for the formal introduction
- Rides in Audiffret's carriage afterward

## Character Description

Marie finds Galula charming and pleasant to talk with. He appears to be well-integrated into the local Nice society and comfortable with social interactions. His role as intermediary suggests he understood both Marie's interest in Audiffret and Audiffret's interest in Marie.

## Significance

Galula's role cannot be overstated - without his intervention, Marie and Audiffret might never have been formally introduced. He serves as the catalyst for their entire relationship.
