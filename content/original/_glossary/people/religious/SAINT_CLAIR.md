# Baron de Saint-Clair

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-14

## Identity
French aristocrat and accomplished marksman who participated in Monaco pigeon shoots.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 14**: Won second prize at the Monaco pigeon shoot. Marie lists him among the shooters assembled at the train station.

## Context
- Part of the elite shooting society that gathered for prestigious competitions
- Regular participant in Monaco's social and sporting events
