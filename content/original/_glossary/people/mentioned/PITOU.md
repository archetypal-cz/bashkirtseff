# Pitou

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-28

## Identity
Male pet dog in the Bashkirtseff household who now has a female companion.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 1**: "Pitou l'a terriblement mordue" - badly bites Marie's mother's hands while she's washing them.
- **February 28**: "On a acheté une femme à Pitou" (They bought a female for Pitou).

## Context
- Male household pet with aggressive tendencies
- Name typical for French dogs
- Incident interrupts mother's potential ball attendance
