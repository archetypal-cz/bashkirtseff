# Visconti

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-06-28
**Diary Coverage**: 1873-02-19

A business establishment in Nice where Marie obtained carnival tickets. The Nice Carnival was (and remains) one of the major social events of the Riviera season, typically held in February before Lent. For Marie's social circle, attending the carnival would have been an important occasion for seeing and being seen.

## Diary References
- **1873-02-19**: Marie and her companions purchase carnival tickets at Visconti's establishment.

## Historical Context
The Nice Carnival in the 1870s was a major aristocratic and bourgeois social event, featuring parades, masked balls, and elaborate festivities. Ticket offices like Visconti would have been important social hubs during carnival season.
