# Mme Narichkine

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
Russian aristocratic woman, aunt of Prince Tchetvertinski. Part of the Russian nobility in Nice.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At Howard dance with her nephew Prince Tchetvertinski: "le prince Tchetvertinski, Mme Narichkine sa tante."

## Context
- Member of prominent Russian noble family
- The Narichkine (Naryshkin) family was one of Russia's most distinguished
- Chaperones her nephew at social events
- Part of Russian aristocratic network in Nice

## Research Notes
- Narichkine/Naryshkin family closely connected to Russian imperial court
- Family produced Tsarina Natalya Naryshkina (mother of Peter the Great)
- Typical for aunts to chaperone young noble relatives
