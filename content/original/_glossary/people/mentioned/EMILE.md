# Emile

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-17

## Identity
An older man in Nice known for his cabriolet (two-wheeled carriage).

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 17**: His cabriolet used as comparison for Wittgenstein's vehicle: "Un cabriolet comme celui du vieux Emile" (A cabriolet like that of old Emile).

## Context
- "Le vieux Emile" - the old Emile
- Owns recognizable cabriolet
- Part of Nice society
- His vehicle represents bourgeois transport
- Used as reference point for shabby vehicles
