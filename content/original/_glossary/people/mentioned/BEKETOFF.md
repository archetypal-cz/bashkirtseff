# Mme Beketoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
Russian woman in Nice society whom Marie once mistook the Viennese girl for.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: Marie tells the Viennese girl: "Un jour la prenant pour Mme Beketoff je l'ai suivie quelques minutes" (One day taking her for Mme Beketoff I followed her for a few minutes).

## Context
- Russian aristocratic woman (Beketoff is noble Russian surname)
- Similar enough in appearance to the Viennese girl to cause confusion
- Known to Marie by sight
- Part of Nice social scene

## Research Notes
- Beketov/Beketoff prominent Russian noble family
- The mistaken identity suggests similar age/bearing to Viennese girl
