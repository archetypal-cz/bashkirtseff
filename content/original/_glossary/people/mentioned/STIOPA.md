# Stiopa

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-28

## Overview

"Uncle Stiopa" (Uncle Stepan) - Russian family member who visits Nice with wife Machenka in January 1874 and remains as regular household member.

## Relationship to Marie

Affectionate uncle who "finds her beautiful because he loves her." Brings Russian delicacies and preserves when visiting.

## Characteristics

- Patriotic - tries to convince Marie that Russia is better than abroad
- Traditional - suggests Marie should marry a Grand Duke
- Teasing - notices and points out when Marie blushes at mention of Hamilton
- Regular gambling participant at casino

## Social Activities

- Stays with Walitsky during Nice visits
- Participates in Russian Christmas Eve traditions
- Engages in family discussions about Russian nobility
- Accompanies family to Monaco casino

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 5**: Christmas visit with wife Machenka
- **March 28**: Goes to Monaco with family party, lunches with Marie and Paul at casino restaurant at 2:30
