# M. Potemkine

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-23

## Identity
Russian gentleman, engaged to Mlle Filimonoff.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 19**: Attends Dina's birthday party. His engagement to Mlle Filimonoff is announced. Marie notes "Elle est très bien, lui pas mal" (She is very good, he's not bad).
- **February 11**: Married to Mlle Filimonoff in Russian Orthodox ceremony with crown exchange
- **February 23**: As "le nouveau marié" (the newlywed), accompanies Marie's group back from Monte-Carlo. They joke about travels in Russia and false reports. At the casino, he's one of the men surrounding Maman along with Berr, another Potemkine, and Bach.

## Social Context
- Part of Russian colony in Nice
- Engagement represents social alliances within expatriate community
- Name suggests possible connection to famous Potemkin family
