# Manderstern

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-01-23
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1880-10-12

Marie's father held the equivalent position for the district (not the entire province), making Manderstern's position hierarchically superior.

In October 1880, Manderstern and Abaza sent Marie a congratulatory telegram on her father's unanimous election to his position. Marie notes the political importance of this gesture - these were influential figures in Poltava society, and she responds with careful diplomatic courtesy, aware that she may someday need to present her own candidacy in Poltava.

**Russian nobility structure:**
- Marshal of nobility for the province (gouvernement) - Manderstern's position
- Marshal of nobility for the district - Marie's father's position
