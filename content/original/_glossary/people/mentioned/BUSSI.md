# Bussi

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-12

## Identity
Jeweler in Nice who sells diamond jewelry and ornaments.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 12**: Marie goes to see "parures en diamants" (diamond sets) at Bussi's shop, satisfying her sudden craving for diamond jewelry that has lasted two days.

## Context
- Luxury jeweler in Nice
- Caters to wealthy clientele including Russian aristocrats
- Represents Marie's material desires and shopping habits
- Part of Nice's commercial district serving the international elite
- Marie's pattern of always wanting something new
