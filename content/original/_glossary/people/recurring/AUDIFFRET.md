## Overview

The Audiffret family was one of the most frequently mentioned families in Marie Bashkirtseff's diary during her time in Nice (1873-1874), with over 40 references. They appear to have been a prominent local French family, part of the established Nice society that mixed with the international winter visitors.

## Family Members

### Young Audiffret
- A young man who showed romantic interest in Marie
- October 12, 1873: Marie notes dismissively that "le petit Audiffret s'occupe de moi" (young Audiffret is interested in me)
- His attentions were not reciprocated by Marie, who was obsessed with the Duke of Hamilton

### The Audiffret Family
- Regular fixtures at social events in Nice
- Present at balls, promenades, and gatherings
- Part of the established French families that formed Nice society
