# Monaco Casino

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-23

## Location
The famous Casino de Monte-Carlo in Monaco, opened in 1863.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 23**: Opening ceremony of the roulette season. Marie describes the crowd as "animaux" (animals) rushing for carriages and eating in the dining hall.

## Social Context
- Major social venue for winter season
- Gambling center attracting international society
- Connected to pigeon shooting grounds
- Formal dinners and social events
- Train service from Nice for day trips
