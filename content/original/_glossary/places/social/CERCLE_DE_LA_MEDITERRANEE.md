# Cercle de la Méditerranée

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-08

## Identity
Elite men's club in Nice with prominent balcony overlooking the Promenade.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 8**: Members "étalés" (spread out) on balcony including Lewin, Krassowsky, Jarochewsky, Lambertye and others watching passersby.

## Context
- Exclusive social club
- Strategic viewing location
- Male-only space for elite society
- Members observe and are observed
- Part of Nice social theater
