# Avenue de la Gare

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-13

## Identity
Street in Nice near the train station where the pastry shop Fai/Fay is located.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 13**: Location of Fai/Fay pastry shop that Marie dreams about: "quelque part près ou dans l'avenue de la Gare."

## Context
- Commercial street near Nice train station
- Home to quality shops including best pastry chef
- Part of newer Nice development (train era)
- "Avenue de la Gare" = Station Avenue

## Research Notes
- Railway arrived in Nice 1864
- Area developed rapidly after train connection
- Now likely Avenue Thiers or Avenue Jean Médecin
