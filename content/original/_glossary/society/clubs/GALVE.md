# Galve

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-28

## Identity
An old man in Nice society who participates in shooting matches and associates with Lambertye's group.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 19**: In Russian passage: "le vieux Galve par exemple" (old Galve for example) - Marie says she looks at Lambertye more than many men, more than old Galve, but not as a man.
- **March 28**: Boards train compartment next to Marie's family with Lambertye, Furstenberg and others going to Monaco shooting match.

- Elderly man ("vieux")
