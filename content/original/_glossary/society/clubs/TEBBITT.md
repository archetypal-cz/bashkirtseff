# Tebbitt Family

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
English family in Nice society. Marie dismisses them as ordinary, not part of the elegant world.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At Howard dance: "il n'y a que des Tebbitt, des Barter et dans ce genre" - only the Tebbitts, the Barters and that type. Marie considers them insufficient for a grand dancing matinee.

## Context
- Part of English colony in Nice
- Not considered "monde élégant" by Marie
- Grouped with Barter family as ordinary English
- Attend same social events but not Marie's preferred society

## Research Notes
- Typical of middle-class English families wintering in Nice
- Name spelling may vary (Tebbitt, Tebbit, etc.)
