%% 036.0006 %%

%% 036.0008 %%

%% 2026-02-10T17:19:00 LAN: "son frere" - her brother; here meaning Audiffret, whom Olga Sapogenikoff treats as an honorary brother (see 036.1875-07-11 where "son frere" is glossed the same way). NOT a biological brother %%
