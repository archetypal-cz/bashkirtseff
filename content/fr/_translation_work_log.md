## Translation Guidelines Summary

**TRANSLATE:**
- Actual foreign phrases/sentences that convey meaning
- Code-switches (even single words like "expectation")
- Common expressions ("Chi lo sa?", "O canaglia!")
- Russian diminutives/terms when they have clear French equivalents

**SKIP (no translation needed):**
- Proper nouns (names: Pietruccio, etc.)
- Titles of works (Orlando Furioso)
- Etymology explanations (Vaticinio)
- French loanwords already established (moujiks)
- Latin maxims that Marie herself translates immediately after
- Truncated references where meaning is unclear

**QUESTIONABLE (case-by-case):**
- Opera/song lyrics (translate if substantive)
- Latin mottos (translate for accessibility)
- Place names in foreign languages when explained in context

## Format for FRE Comments

```
%% 2026-02-14T19:00:00 FRE: "original text" — traduit de l'[langue] %%
```

```

```
