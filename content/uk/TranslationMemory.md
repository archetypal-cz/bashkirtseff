## Stylistic Conventions
- Use «» for quoted speech
- Em dash — for dialogue and parenthetical insertions
- Dates: 11 листопада 1859 року
- Keep "верст" for verstes (Russian distance unit)
- Keep "льє" for lieues (French distance unit)
- Keep "карбованці" for roubles (period currency)
- Keep French terms like *document humain* in italics on first use
- Marie = Марі (French form, since she wrote in French and identified as "Marie")
- Footnote markers: [^XX.YY.Z] format
