%% 2026-02-09T17:00:00 RSR: Marie's literary manifesto. Marie's rejection of Sevigne's "affected" and "wrought" style in favor of raw spontaneity is both audacious (challenging the canon) and strategic (justifying the diary's artlessness as a literary choice, not a deficiency).
