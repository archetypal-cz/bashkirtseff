## Key Terms

### toilette
- Translation: dress / outfit / ensemble
- Context: 1870s usage = the complete outfit, NOT "toilet"
- Notes: "Change one's toilette" → "change one's dress"

### en amazone
- Translation: in a riding habit / sidesaddle
- Context: Women's riding costume with long skirt for sidesaddle
- Notes: NOT "like an Amazon"

### canaille
- Translation: scoundrel / rascal / rogue
- Context: Pejorative for lower classes; Marie uses it dramatically
- Notes: Context-dependent — sometimes keep French in italics

### promenade
- Translation: promenade / walk
- Context: Both the place and the social activity of walking
- Notes: Keep "promenade" when referring to the specific location
