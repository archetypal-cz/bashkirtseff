At the Cercle Masséna,[^01.21.1] an enormous crowd. Maman danced. In the evening, at the French Theatre, *Les martyrs de l'inquisition*, by Méran. Boreel approached me, but I felt almost nothing... Strange!

%% 2026-02-13T18:00:05 RED: EXCELLENT. Para 001.0021. "I felt almost nothing... Strange!" — the ellipsis and single-word exclamation perfectly capture Marie's self-analytical brevity. Cercle Masséna footnote adds useful context. Score: 0.90 %%

[^01.21.1]: The Cercle Masséna was named after André Masséna, one of Napoleon's marshals who was born in Nice. It was an important social institution where the local aristocracy and wealthy visitors gathered for balls, concerts, and other events.
