# Wednesday, 5 February 1873

%% 001.0082 %%
%% [#Nice](../../_original/_glossary/places/cities/NICE.md) [#Var_Races](../../_original/_glossary/people/mentioned/VAR_RACES.md) %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:19:00 TR: "la saison" → "the season" per LAN — the Nice winter social season %%
%% Le jour des courses du Var. Le plus grand jour de la saison pour moi. %%
The day of the Var races. The greatest day of the season for me.

%% 001.0083 %%
%% Le matin sont passés devant la villa une dizaine de chevaux conduits par des jockeys. Mon Dieu, qu'y a-t-il de plus beau qu'un cheval de course ? %%
In the morning, about ten horses passed before the villa, led by jockeys. My God, what is more beautiful than a racehorse?

%% 001.0084 %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:19:30 TR: "monde très élégant" → "very elegant society" per LAN — "monde" = fashionable world %%
%% A une heure de l'après-midi me voici aux courses. Enor­mément de monde très élégant. %%
At one in the afternoon, here I am at the races. An enormous crowd, very elegant.

%% 001.0085 %%
%% [#Howard_family](../../_original/_glossary/people/recurring/HOWARD_FAMILY.md) [#Gagarine](../../_original/_glossary/places/cities/GAGARINE.md) %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:20:00 TR: "Gagarine" → "Gagarin" per TM (anglicized, no final -e); "la tribune" → "the grandstand" per LAN; "parié" → "bet" — social betting with bonbons %%
%% Je me suis promenée un moment, puis à la tribune, j'ai parlé avec les Howard, avec Gagarine; j'ai parié et j'ai perdu une boite de bonbons. %%
I walked about for a while, then at the grandstand I spoke with the Howards, with Gagarin; I bet and lost a box of bonbons.

%% 001.0086 %%
%% [#Kernberger](../../_original/_glossary/people/writers/KATHERINE_KERNBERGER.md) %%
%% [#Duke_of_Hamilton](../../_original/_glossary/people/core/DUKE_OF_HAMILTON.md) %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:20:30 TR: "commissaire aux courses" → "race commissioner" per LAN; "le plus beau monde" → "the finest society" per LAN %%

The Duke is race commissioner,[^01.86.1] yet I did not see him. I was in the grandstand with the finest society of Nice.

%% 001.0087 %%
%% [#Kernberger](../../_original/_glossary/people/writers/KATHERINE_KERNBERGER.md) %%

I saw Boreel; he was very well dressed, but he is less handsome than the Duke. He is decidedly too stout.

%% 001.0088 %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:21:30 TR: "mais je l'aime, lui, voilà !" — emphatic doubled pronoun per LAN; rendered as "that's all there is to it" to capture declarative finality %%
%% Cependant, le duc est plus gros que lui, mais je l'aime, lui, voilà ! %%
And yet, the Duke is stouter than him — but I love him, that's all there is to it!

%% 001.0089 %%
%% [#Gioia](../../_original/_glossary/places/cities/GIOIA.md) %%
%% 2026-02-13T17:22:00 TR: "cette femme" → "that woman" — consistent dismissive per LAN %%
%% Ah ! s'il pouvait abandonner cette femme ! Je l'ai vue en noir dans une voiture fermée, seule; elle n'est pas venue aux courses. Où est-elle ? %%
Ah! if only he would abandon that woman! I saw her in black, in a closed carriage, alone; she did not come to the races. Where is she?

%% 001.0090 %%
%% [#TIR_AUX_PIGEONS](../../_original/_glossary/people/mentioned/TIR_AUX_PIGEONS.md) %%
