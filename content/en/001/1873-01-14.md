%% 2026-02-13T17:05:00 TR: "jolies toilettes" → "beautiful dresses" per TM; "notre loge" → "our box"; "ne m'amuse plus" → "no longer amuses me" per LAN; "Et dire que" → "And to think" — idiomatic; "sa démarche" → "his gait" per LAN %%
%% Ce bal Floréal a amené beaucoup de monde, mais pas de jolies toilettes. Moi, en robe rose. Un grand nombre de per­sonnes sont venues nous voir dans notre loge. Boreel ne m'amu­se plus beaucoup. Et dire qu'il m'a tellement intéressée ! Au fond je crois que c'est sa démarche qui me déplaît. %%
The Floréal ball drew a great crowd, but no beautiful dresses. I wore a pink gown. A great many people came to see us in our box. Boreel no longer amuses me much. And to think how he once fascinated me! Deep down, I believe it is his gait that displeases me.

%% 2026-02-13T18:00:03 RED: EXCELLENT. Para 001.0016. "And to think how he once fascinated me!" captures Marie's dramatic fickleness. "Deep down, I believe it is his gait that displeases me" — the shift from adoration to criticism over a walk is quintessentially Marie. Score: 0.90 %%

[^01.16.1]: The Floréal balls were significant social events in Nice during the winter season, attended by both local aristocracy and wealthy visitors.
