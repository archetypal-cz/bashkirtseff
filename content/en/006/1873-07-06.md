# Sunday, 6 July 1873

%% 006.0199 %%
%% [#Kernberger](../../_original/_glossary/people/writers/KATHERINE_KERNBERGER.md) %%
%% [#Religion](../../_original/_glossary/culture/themes/RELIGION.md) %%
%% 2026-02-16T11:40:00 TR: Russian church under repair until October. Marie vows not to wet her hair anymore — water darkens it. "Excepté sans doute quelques cas extraordinaires" — except certain extraordinary cases where "someone" might be present; Hamilton is implied but left unsaid. %%
%% L'eglise russe est en reparation et l'on n'officie plus jusqu'en octobre. Vers quatre heures nous allames au bain de mer. Je me suis jetee du pont et du bateau pour montrer a maman et je promets a moi-meme et aux autres de ne plus mouiller mes cheveux, l'eau les rend fonces et cela me desole. Je donne donc ma parole d'honneur de ne pas les mouiller, excepte sans doute quelques cas extraordinaires ou je serai en societe ou il y aurait quelqu'un de... mais a Nice en ete, je ne me jetterai plus du pont. %%
The Russian church is under repair and there are no more services until October. Around four we went for a sea bath. I threw myself from the pier and the boat to show Maman, and I promise myself and the others not to wet my hair anymore — the water makes it dark, which distresses me. I give my word of honour not to wet it — except, of course, in certain extraordinary cases where I shall be in company where there might be someone who... But in Nice, in summer, I shall no longer throw myself from the pier.

After dinner, Maman, Dina, *Marie Bogdanovna*, and Papa went to see the procession. I stayed behind because Paul was to come for me in a cab. There were two of us; the carriage was quite good and the horse very fast. This procession is rather fine, but I am no judge, for I detest all these comedies. It is a sacrilege, to my mind. They carry the Holy Virgin through the whole town. In my opinion, even images and statues of the saints are a sin. It is against my religion — but I can see the errors in my own as well. May God forgive me! But my faith is true, honest, pure.
