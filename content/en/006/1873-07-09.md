It has been three days since I last wrote my diary; I shall try to remember Wednesday. I no longer know — nothing extraordinary; in the evening with the Anitchkoffs at Rumpelmayer's. We are going to Vienna on Saturday. I am happy, but Maman is staying behind, and that is vexing. There is no pleasure without pain. That is a great truth.
