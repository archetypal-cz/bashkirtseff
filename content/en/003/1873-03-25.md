# Tuesday, 25 March 1873

%% 003.0099 %%
%% [#Nice](../../_original/_glossary/places/cities/NICE.md) %%

Sunshine, but dust and wind.

%% 003.0100 %%
%% [#Boreel](../../_original/_glossary/people/recurring/BOREEL.md) %%
%% 2026-02-13T19:00:13 TR: "fortifier son délaissement" → "strengthen his abandonment" — Marie's self-conscious writing about Boreel. "devant Dieu" → "before God" per LAN. %%
%% Quoique je ne devais pas en parler mais je veux dire que maintenant quand je vois Boreel, il est pour moi comme tout le monde et absolument rien de plus, je ne devais pas en parler, mais puisque c'est pour fortifier son délaissement. Et puis je dis cela, pour que, si je lirai ce journal plus tard, je ne crois que j'ai souffert pour Boreel. Il m'est indifférent devant Dieu. %%
Although I should not speak of it — but I want to say that now when I see Boreel, he is for me like everyone else and absolutely nothing more. I should not speak of it, but since it is to strengthen his abandonment. And then I say this so that if I read this journal later, I do not think I suffered for Boreel. He is indifferent to me before God.

Today, while we were at dinner, and during my lesson, I thought I heard the sound of his carriage — alas, I could not make certain. How I wish he would come! Let him leave that woman — if she were his alone and if he left her, it could make her unhappy, but it is far from being so. I wish her no harm, if only because he loves her!
