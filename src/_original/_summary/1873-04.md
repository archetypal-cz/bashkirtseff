# April 1873 Summary

## Key Events and Themes

### The Duke of Hamilton's Absence
- Marie continues to pine for the Duke who remains absent throughout April
- She has vivid visions of him during Good Friday church service (April 18)
- Dreams of meeting him at the Vienna World Exhibition
- Discovers Gioia has left for Russia regarding inheritance matters, which gives her hope

### Family Conflicts
- Ongoing issues with governess Mlle Collignon's teaching methods and neglect of lessons
- Major confrontation with the Tolstoy family (April 24) who attempt to enter their home after 8 months of feuding
- Mother falls ill after the Tolstoy incident, has an attack (April 27)

### Religious Observance
- Orthodox Holy Week (April 14-20) with daily church attendance
- Confession on April 19 where priest calls her vain
- Easter midnight mass and celebrations
- Marie struggles to pray properly in church, finds it easier at home alone

### Social Life and Appearance
- Constant wardrobe anxieties - winter clothes too heavy, no suitable spring clothes
- Orders new dresses from Paris and copies Mlle de Galve's Italian straw hat style
- Attends pigeon shooting competitions at Monaco, discovers genuine love for the sport
- Sees Lord Carlos Hamilton (the Duke's brother) multiple times

### Romantic Developments
- Boreel fades completely from her affections
- Mother begins pushing Gritsia Miloradovitch as a marriage prospect
- Dramatic scenes when Mlle Collignon spots her former lover with another woman (May 1)

### Literary and Philosophical Moments
- Reads Mrs. Henry Wood's novel about working persistently toward goals with God's blessing
- Continues intense religious faith and prayers for the Duke
- Strange shared dreams with others about drowning and world ending

## Notable Quotes

"Il était avec moi, d'ailleurs il ne me quitte jamais, il est toujours devant mes yeux." (April 27)

"C'est pour la première fois que j'aime véritablement!" (April 18)

"Je dois être ou la duchesse de Hamilton... ou recherchée, connue, estimée, courue après, spoken of, vivre au milieu des fêtes de la plus haute société" (April 25)

## Health and Well-being
- Marie rises at 5:30 AM, in bed by 10 PM
- Takes sea baths
- Increasingly frustrated with Nice and desperate to leave for proper education in Florence, Rome, etc.
- Family trapped in Nice by ongoing Russian lawsuit

## Social Observations
- Nice society declining as season ends
- Princess Souvoroff's satisfied air comes from wealth and getting everything she desires
- Audiffret now "cock of the village" but being ruined by Gioia

## Future Plans
- Hopes to attend Vienna World Exhibition
- Considers moves to Italy
- Dreams of life in high society or as stage celebrity (prefers the former)
