# A Midsummer Night's Dream (Le songe d'une nuit d'été)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-05-02

## Identity
Shakespeare's comedy, performed as an opera or musical adaptation at Nice theater. Marie finds it boring and lacking intrigue.

- **May 2**: Attends performance in Gioia's box. "La salle insignifiante, la pièce ennuyeuse et dénuée d'intérêt et d'intrigue." Tenor playing Shakespeare resembles Duc de Mouchy exactly.
