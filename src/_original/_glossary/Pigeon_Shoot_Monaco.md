# Monaco Pigeon Shoot (Tir aux Pigeons)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-23

## Event
Elite sporting event at Monte Carlo involving live pigeon shooting competitions.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 23**: Final day of shooting season. M. Blanc hosts dinner for all shooters. Fireworks display with "1874 - Sir W Call" illuminated on lawn. Princess Souvoroff sits fearlessly near barrier despite shot pellets. Marie plans to host the shooters' dinner herself in three years.

### 1873
- Multiple references to attending shoots in Monaco

## Social Context
- Major society event alongside races
- Entry fee of 20 francs considered expensive
- Elite participants and spectators
- "Poule de consolation" (consolation prize round)
- Formal dinners for participants
- Ladies watch from safe areas due to shot pellets
- Symbol of Marie's social ambitions
