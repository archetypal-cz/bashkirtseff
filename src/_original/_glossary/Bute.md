# Bute

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-06

## Identity
A name that fascinates Marie, similar to how Hamilton's name affected her.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 6**: Marie mentions being fascinated by the name "Bute" just as she always was by "Hamilton": "comme à présent le nom de Bute du premier moment il me plut et je le trouve extraordinaire et fascinant. Bute!"

## Context
- Likely refers to Marquess of Bute
- Scottish aristocratic title
- Name has immediate appeal to Marie
- Compared to Hamilton in terms of fascination
- Exclamation "Bute!" shows enthusiasm
- Part of Marie's attraction to British nobility
