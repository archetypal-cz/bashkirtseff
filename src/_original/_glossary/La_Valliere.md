# La Vallière (Book)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-01

## Literary Work
Book by Arsène Houssaye about Louise de La Vallière, mistress of Louis XIV.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 19**: Marie reading while sick, notes "J'aime le duc parce qu'il a l'air d'un duc" (I love the duke because he looks like a duke)
- **February 1**: Finishes the book. Finds Houssaye monotonous but reads with pleasure, "se lit à vapeur" (reads quickly)

### 1873
- Multiple references to reading about La Vallière

## Context
- Part of Houssaye's series on royal mistresses
- Marie fascinated by court life and aristocracy
- Feeds her romantic ideals about nobility
- Part of her self-education in French history
