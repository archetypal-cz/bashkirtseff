# Laferrière

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-21

## Identity
Fashion house or couturier that Marie considers as an alternative to Worth for her clothing needs.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 21**: Marie decides "de ne plus aller chez Worth, mais chez Laferrière et Querteux. Worth est trop massif et sévère."

## Context
- Alternative fashion house to Worth
- Presumably offers a lighter, less severe style than Worth
- Part of the competitive Paris fashion industry
- Paired with Querteux as Marie's new choice
- Represents Marie's evolving fashion preferences away from Worth's "massif et sévère" aesthetic
