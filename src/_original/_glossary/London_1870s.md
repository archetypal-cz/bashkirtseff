**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05

## Marie's References

### 1874
- **January 14**: In her fantasy of adult life, Marie imagines living in Paris or London "selon les circonstances" (according to circumstances) and coming to Nice for a month to frolic with a chosen "bataclan" (rowdy group).

## Historical Context
- Capital of the British Empire at its height
- Center of international finance and commerce
- Hub of artistic and intellectual life
- Popular with wealthy international elite
- Season ran from Easter to July
- Many Russians maintained residences

## Society Life
- Strict social protocols and calling cards
- Presentation at Court for debutantes
- Opera, theater, and concert seasons
- Hyde Park rides and promenades
- Grand balls and dinner parties

## For Marie's Class
- International aristocracy mingled freely
- Less rigid than British society for foreigners
- Shopping at Liberty, Harrods, and Bond Street
- Cultural attractions and museums
- Base for European travel
