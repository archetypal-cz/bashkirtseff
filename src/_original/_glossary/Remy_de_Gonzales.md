# Rémy de Gonzalès

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-05-03

## Identity
Young man from Baden-Baden who attempted romantic advances with Marie three years earlier (1871) when she was 12.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **May 3**: Marie recalls Baden incident from 3 years ago: "une espèce de bêtise avec Rémy de Gonzalès." During hide-and-seek when candle went out, he held her hand. When he tried to kiss her cheek "avec un grand soupir," she maneuvered so he kissed her hand instead. He "devinait ma pensée" (divined her thoughts). At train station departure to Stuttgart, asked for her flower as souvenir "les larmes aux yeux." She gave it dismissively: "Quelle bêtise."

## Context
- Spanish/French surname suggests noble background
