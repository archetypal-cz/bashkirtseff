# Pigalle

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-18

## Identity
Hat maker or milliner in Nice who creates fashionable headwear for Marie and her family.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 18**: Marie orders a hat from Pigalle while out shopping in Nice.

## Context
- Fashion vendor in Nice
- Serves the international clientele including Russian aristocrats
- Part of Nice's luxury goods industry
- Creates custom millinery for wealthy visitors
- Represents Marie's continuing attention to fashion and appearance despite family tensions
