# Youlia Stepanovna

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
Russian woman whom Marie calls "la sorcière" (the witch). Gives Marie disapproving looks at social events.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At Howard dance, when Woerman is attentive to Marie: "la *sorcière Youlia Stepanovna* en passant me regarda tellement quand ce fou était à côté de moi" (the witch Youlia Stepanovna passing by looked at me so much when this madman was beside me).

## Context
- Russian (patronymic Stepanovna = daughter of Stepan)
- First name Youlia = Julia
- Marie's epithet "sorcière" suggests:
  - Older woman
  - Disapproving/judgmental
  - Perhaps gossips about young people
- Monitors young people's behavior at social events
