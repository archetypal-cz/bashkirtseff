# Piccon

**Research Status**: Moderate
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-28

## Identity
Nice separatist politician who gave controversial speech advocating Nice's separation from France. Marie names her new white dog after him.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 28**: "ce Piccon a prononcé un discours dans je ne sais quelle assemblée, qui disait que Nice doit se séparer de la France etc." Forced to resign. Famous in Paris, Lyon, Marseille and Nice. Marie names Lewita's former white dog "M. Piccon" - "nom court et agréable pour un chien."

## Historical Context
- Nice annexed by France 1860
- Separatist movements persisted
- Political tensions 1870s
- Local autonomy debates

## Dog Connection
- White dog formerly owned by Lewita
- Paul obtained it for Marie
- Lewita claimed gave it to her beauty
