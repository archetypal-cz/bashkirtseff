# King of Hanover

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-13

## Identity
The last King of Hanover, whose son visits Nice in February 1874. Hanover was annexed by Prussia in 1866, ending the kingdom.

## Context
- Deposed royal family living in exile after 1866
- Part of international aristocracy wintering in Nice
- Son represents fallen German royalty
- Marie's immediate curiosity about royal visitors
- Disappointment when reality doesn't match expectations
