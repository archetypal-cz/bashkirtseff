# Fedoroff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-14

## Identity
Russian expatriate in Nice who died suddenly while playing cards.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 14**: Dies of aneurysm while playing cards at Patton's house: "M. Fedoroff est passé de vie à trépas hier soir en jouant aux cartes chez M. Patton" (M. Fedoroff passed from life to death yesterday evening while playing cards at M. Patton's). Marie notes: "Quelle surprise pour les maîtres de la maison" (What a surprise for the hosts).
