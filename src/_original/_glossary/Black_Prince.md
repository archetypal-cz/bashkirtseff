# Black Prince

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-20

## Identity
A riding horse used by Marie for her morning rides with Paul in Nice.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 20**: Marie rides Black Prince from 11 AM to 1 PM: "Black Prince, ce pauvre cheval est très tranquille, adroit, on l'a monté au Luxembourg" (Black Prince, this poor horse is very calm, skillful, he was ridden at Luxembourg).

## Context
- Gentle, well-trained horse ("tranquille")
- Skillful ("adroit")
- Previously ridden at Luxembourg (Paris)
- Suitable for leisurely rides
- Marie rides during hottest part of day, gets sunburned
