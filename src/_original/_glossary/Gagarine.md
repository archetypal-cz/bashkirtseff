## Overview

The Gagarine family (also spelled Gagarin) was part of the Russian aristocratic circle in Nice during the 1870s. With 20 mentions in Marie Bashkirtseff's diary, they were regular participants in the social life of the Russian expatriate community.

## Historical Background

The Gagarines were:
- **Ancient Russian Nobility**: One of the oldest princely families of Russia
- **Rurikid Dynasty**: Descended from the medieval rulers of Russia
- **Prominent in Imperial Service**: Family members often held high positions
- **International Presence**: Like many Russian nobles, maintained residences abroad
