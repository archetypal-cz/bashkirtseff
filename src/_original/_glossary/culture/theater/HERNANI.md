## Identity

### 1874
- **February 4**: Scheduled for Saturday performance with "la Vigier" (likely actress performing the female lead).

## Historical Context
- Landmark of French Romantic theater
- Story of outlawed nobleman in love with Doña Sol
- Famous for breaking classical unities
- Caused "Battle of Hernani" between romantics and classicists in 1830
- Still performed regularly in 1870s
- Lead role of Doña Sol was a showcase for actresses
