# Dis-moi Vénus

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-09

## Identity
Song from Offenbach's "La Belle Hélène" that Marie performs.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 9**: "J'ai chanté Dis-moi Vénus en costume et par-devant la glace" (I sang "Tell me Venus" in costume and in front of the mirror).

## Context
- Famous aria from Act I of La Belle Hélène
- Sung by Helen of Troy character
- Full title: "Dis-moi, Vénus, quel plaisir trouves-tu"
- Marie performs in costume before mirror
- Private theatrical performance
- Shows her musical training and dramatic interests
