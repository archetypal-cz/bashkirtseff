# Rue Saint-François

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-12

## Identity
A street in Nice where Marie encounters Prince Tchetvertinski, Mlle de Galve, and M. Zveguinzoff riding horses.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 12**: Marie sees the riding party: "je les ai rencontrés à la rue Saint-François et même dans notre ruelle" (I met them on rue Saint-François and even in our alley).

## Context
- Street in Nice's old town
- Part of riding circuit for society members
- Close enough to Bashkirtseff residence ("notre ruelle")
- Named after Saint Francis

## Research Notes
- Rue Saint-François-de-Paule likely reference
- Major street in Vieux Nice (Old Nice)
- Near Place Masséna and opera house
