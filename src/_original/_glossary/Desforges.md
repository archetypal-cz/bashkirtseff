# Desforges

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-24

## Identity

Real estate agent or property broker in Nice involved in the Bashkirtseff family's purchase of Villa Carlone in 1874.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 24**: Mentioned in connection with the villa purchase on rue de la Préfecture - "*il faut aller chez Desforges acheter la villa*" [must go to Desforges to buy the villa]

## Context

Desforges appears to have been handling the sale of Villa Carlone to the Bashkirtseff family. The transaction involved a property valued at 218,000 francs located on rue de la Préfecture in Nice. His role suggests he was either a notary, real estate agent, or property broker facilitating high-value transactions among the wealthy expatriate community in Nice during the 1870s.
