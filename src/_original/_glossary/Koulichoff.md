# Mme Koulichoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-09

## Identity
Russian married woman in Nice society. Marie finds her appearance unattractive.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 11**: Present at Filimonoff Russian Orthodox wedding, seated with Mmes Potemkine and Comtesse Tolstoï on the right side (groom's side) in the first row.
- **March 5**: Comes to visit the new villa (Villa Carlone).
- **March 9**: In Marie's nightmare, Hamilton's wife described as mixture of "Hitchcock, de Mme Koulichoff et de la Gaugiran" - Marie's reference for unattractiveness.

## Context
- Part of Russian Orthodox wedding ceremony
- Member of Russian aristocratic community in Nice
- Associated with groom's family or close friends
- Social equal to Comtesse Tolstoï and Mme Potemkine
- Marie uses her as negative aesthetic reference point
