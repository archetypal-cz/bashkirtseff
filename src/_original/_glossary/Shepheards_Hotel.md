# Shepheard's Hotel

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-22

## Identity
Famous British colonial hotel in Cairo where the Duke of Hamilton stayed during his Egyptian travels.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 22**: Hamilton and Mandeville "ont logé à l'hôtel Sheapheards" (stayed at Shepheard's Hotel) in Cairo.

## Context
- Premier British hotel in Cairo
- Center of colonial social life
- Luxury accommodation
- Meeting place for aristocrats
- Symbol of British Egypt

## Historical Context
- Opened 1841
- Terrace overlooking Ezbekieh Gardens
- Destroyed 1952 (Cairo fire)
- Height of luxury travel
