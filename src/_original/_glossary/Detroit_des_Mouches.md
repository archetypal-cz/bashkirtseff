# Détroit des Mouches

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-27

## Location
Specific area in Nice where certain society members congregate.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 27**: The Labanoff family "seem nailed" there, always with escort of Lambertye, Woerman and another. The daughter described poetically with "loving mouth that goes from ear to ear."

## Context
- "Détroit" means strait or narrow passage
- "Mouches" means flies
- Could be nickname for social gathering spot
- Regular haunt for certain social groups
