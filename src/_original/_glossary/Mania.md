# Mania

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-27

## Identity
Charming young woman, sister of Katia who married Potemkine.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 27**: Marie describes her: "Mania est une charmante fille" (Mania is a charming girl). Tells story of sister's engagement to Potemkine, including how he cried, courted her, and kissed Katia's hand by a window.

## Context
- Russian diminutive name (Maria?)
- Sister to Katia (now Mme Potemkine)
- Good storyteller
- Part of Russian social circle in Nice
- Witness to sister's romantic engagement
