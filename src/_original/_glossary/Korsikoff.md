# Mme Korsikoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-14

## Identity
Russian woman in Nice society whom Marie particularly dislikes.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 14**: At the Monaco pigeon shoot, Marie describes her as "ce gros cocher" (that fat coachman) who came to "grimace and cry in her ugly French." She repeatedly exclaimed "Pauvre pigeon, pauvre pigeon!" (Poor pigeon, poor pigeon!) about the birds being shot.
