# Charles

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-08

## Identity
A young man whom Marie calls "mon Charles" (my Charles). She apparently damaged his hat and jokingly proposes that Dina should give him a new one as part of their betting game.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 8**: In betting game, Marie offers Dina 4000 francs to "donner un chapeau tube neuf au petit, à mon Charles, pour celui que je lui ai abîmé si cruellement" (give a new top hat to the little one, to my Charles, for the one I cruelly ruined).

## Context
- Called "le petit" (the little one) - suggests youth or small stature
- Marie calls him "mon Charles" - possessive suggests familiarity
- The damaged hat incident implies previous interaction
- "Cruellement" (cruelly) is likely Marie's dramatic exaggeration
- Part of Marie's social circle in Nice
