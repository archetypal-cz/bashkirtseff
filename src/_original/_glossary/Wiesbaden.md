## Historical Context

Wiesbaden was a major spa destination for European nobility and wealthy Russians in the 19th century. The Kursaal served as the social center with gambling, music, and elegant dining.

## Significance

The visit marks an important social moment where Marie's beauty creates quite a stir, and where she learns the devastating news about Carlo Hamilton's near-fatal accident.
