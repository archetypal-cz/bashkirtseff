# Nejentsov

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-11

## Identity
Russian man who appears to be inappropriately attached to Paul, Marie's 14-year-old brother.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 11**: Suspected of sending a bouquet of daisies through Paul. "Un certain Nejentsov qui ne quitte pas Paul" (A certain Nejentsov who doesn't leave Paul's side). Family puzzled by his interest in a young boy: "Nous ne comprenons pas non plus quel charme il peut trouver dans la société d'un garçonnet comme Paul" (We don't understand what charm he can find in the company of a young boy like Paul).

## Context
- Russian expatriate in Nice
- Constantly with Paul ("ne quitte pas")
- Suspected of using Paul to send flowers to Marie
- Walitsky suggests interest in Moussia and Dina
- Family finds his attachment to Paul inappropriate
- Part of Paul's concerning social circle
