# La Belle Hélène

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-07

## Identity
Jacques Offenbach's operetta about Helen of Troy, premiered 1864.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 7**: "Je raffole en ce moment de 'La Belle Hélène'. Je la chante et je la joue. 'Au cabaret du labyrinthe etc...'" (I'm crazy about "La Belle Hélène" right now. I sing it and play it. "At the labyrinth cabaret etc...")

## Context
- Comic operetta by Offenbach
- Premiered December 17, 1864 in Paris
- Satirizes Second Empire society through Greek mythology
- "Au cabaret du labyrinthe" - famous drinking song
- Popular with young women for its witty, slightly risqué humor
- Marie plays and sings excerpts
