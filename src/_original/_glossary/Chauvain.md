# Chauvain

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-13

## Identity
One of the principal hotels in Nice during the 1870s.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 13**: Marie and the Princess go to Hotel Chauvain seeking special train tickets for Monaco, but arrive too late. They learn tickets can be purchased at the first-class waiting room entrance.

## Context
- Part of Nice's luxury hotel infrastructure serving wealthy winter residents
- Secretariat sold special event tickets
- Located along the regular evening promenade route
