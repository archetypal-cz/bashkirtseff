# "Mlle de La Vallière"

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-19

## Book Details
Historical work by Arsène Houssaye about Louise de La Vallière (1644-1710), mistress of Louis XIV.

## Marie's References

### 1874
- **January 19**: Begins reading. Notes Houssaye "says everything I think, what I approve but what I cannot express." Quotes: "Elle aimait le roi parce qu'il avait l'air d'un roi" (She loved the king because he looked like a king).

## Marie's Application
- Applies to Hamilton: "J'aime le duc parce qu'il a l'air d'un duc et non parce qu'il est un duc" (I love the duke because he looks like a duke and not because he is a duke)
- Shows her attraction to aristocratic bearing over mere titles
- Plans to devote all free time to this "entraining and interesting" reading

## Historical Context
- La Vallière was Louis XIV's mistress from 1661-1667
- Known for genuine love rather than ambition
- Later became a Carmelite nun
- Her story of love, power, and renunciation fascinates Marie

## Significance
- Feeds Marie's fantasies about court life
- Provides model of woman who captured king through genuine feeling
- Reinforces Marie's belief in power of authentic nobility
