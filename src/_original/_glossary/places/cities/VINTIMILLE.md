# Vintimille

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-15

## Identity
French-Italian border town (Ventimiglia in Italian) where the Bashkirtseff family's Genoa trip ended in chaos.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 15**: Family travels from Nice to Vintimille arguing and crying. At the station, indecision about continuing to Genoa. Stiopa and Machenka depart while Marie's mother refuses to continue. Family returns to Nice.

## Context
- Border town between France and Italy
- Italian: Ventimiglia
- Railway junction point
- First stop in Italy from Nice
- Site of travel decisions
- "Les Italiens sont obligeants" - Italian hospitality noted
