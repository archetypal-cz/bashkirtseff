# England

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-01
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1873-05-03

England appears in the diary primarily in connection with the Duke of Hamilton and English visitors to Nice.

## Key references:
- **May 3, 1873**: Marie sees an English flag on a yacht at Nice port with a ducal coronet, causing her to wonder if it belongs to the Duke of Hamilton
- The Promenade des Anglais in Nice was named after the English community who funded its construction
- Many wealthy English families wintered in Nice during this period

## Cultural context:
Nice was a popular destination for British aristocracy and wealthy families seeking a mild winter climate. The English community was substantial enough to have their own church, clubs, and social institutions.
