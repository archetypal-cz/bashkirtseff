# Hôtel de Paris Monte-Carlo

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-17

## Overview
Luxury hotel in Monte-Carlo, opened in 1864, adjacent to the casino.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 17**: The Bashkirtseff party has lunch there during the rainy pigeon shoot day. Marie gambles at the casino before and after the shooting competition.

## Historical Context
- Opened by Société des Bains de Mer in 1864
- Premier hotel for casino visitors and winter residents
- Symbol of Monte-Carlo luxury and glamour
- Restaurant was social gathering spot during events

## Significance
- Where elite society gathered during sporting events
- Combined dining, gambling, and social networking
- Part of the Monaco/Monte-Carlo entertainment complex
