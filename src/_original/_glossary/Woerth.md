# Woerth

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-18

## Identity
Race horse competing at Nice races in 1874.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 18**: Marie bets on Woerth who falls "comme mort" (like dead) at the last jump along with two other horses. None cleared the final barrier. When lifted, the horse had blood on neck and chest. Marie and others rush to see the injured horse.
