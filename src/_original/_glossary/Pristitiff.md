# Mme Pristitiff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-17

## Identity
Woman acquaintance whom Marie planned to engage with playfully during carnival festivities.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 17**: Marie prepares a bouquet with a note "Pourquoi riez-vous?" (Why are you laughing?) intended for Mme Pristitiff at the prefecture during carnival, but doesn't see her due to the intense flower battle.

## Context
- Part of Marie's social circle in Nice
- Someone Marie feels comfortable enough with to plan a playful interaction
- Present at carnival festivities at the prefecture
- Represents Marie's desire for social connection during public celebrations
- Example of Marie's preparation and anticipation for social encounters
