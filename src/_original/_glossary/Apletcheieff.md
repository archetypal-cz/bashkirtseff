# Mme d'Apletcheieff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
Russian aristocratic woman in Nice society, part of the elegant world Marie values.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At Howard dance, enters with "la Viennoise." Introduces Chevalier Hidalgo to Marie's mother at his insistent request. Marie asks her mother to request introduction to the Viennese girl through Mme d'Apletcheieff.

## Context
- Russian aristocrat (d' prefix indicates nobility)
- Part of "monde élégant" - the elegant world
- Social connector who makes introductions
- Associated with diplomatic circles (introduces Hidalgo)
- Acts as social intermediary

## Research Notes
- Name variations: Apletcheïeff, Apletcheieff
- Russian noble family name
- Important social position as introducer
