# Prater

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-04

## Identity
Marie's large dog who accompanied her in Paris and was well-known in society.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 4**: At the Howards, a gentleman asks "ce qu'est devenu le grand chien qui me suivait toujours, il l'avait remarqué encore à Paris." Lucarini also knows Prater. "Je ne savais pas que Prater fut si connu, on l'admire."

## Context
- Was Marie's constant companion in Paris
- Well-remembered by Nice society
- Admired by many people
- No longer with Marie by 1874
- Shows Marie's distinctive presence in society (girl with the big dog)
