# Smirnoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-10
**Diary Coverage**: Up to July 1, 1875

A gentleman in the Nice Russian social circle who appears at various gatherings and social events.

## Key Appearances

- **June 14, 1875**: Brought from Monte Carlo without a hat, stays at Sapogenikoff house
- **July 1, 1875**: Present at evening gathering, goes to "antichambe verte" with the young ladies

## Social Context

Part of the Russian expatriate community in Nice. The detail about arriving "without a hat" suggests either informality or possible gambling losses (as no hotel would receive someone improperly dressed).

## Character Notes

Described as someone who would not be received at any hotel without proper attire ("sans chapeau, comme une canaille"), suggesting either financial difficulties or social impropriety.

## Social Activities

- Participates in evening entertainments at the Bashkirtseff household
- Associates with the younger generation (Sapogenikoff sisters)
- Present during loud, boisterous gatherings that embarrass Marie

## Related People

- Sapogenikoff family (hosts him)
- Marie Bashkirtseff (observes his behavior)
- Dina (present at gatherings)

## Related Places

- Monte Carlo (where he was brought from)
- Sapogenikoff house (where he stays)
- Nice social circle (regular participant)
