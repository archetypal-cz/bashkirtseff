**Research Status**: Basic

## Identity
Woman in Marie's social circle who went on carriage ride with Machenka and a violin-playing gentleman.

- **March 21**: Machenka tells Marie about going in open carriage with Mme Hamsley and a gentleman who played violin throughout 4-hour journey.
