# Pavloucha

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-19

## Identity
Person in Marie's household who notices when she's upset about Lambertye not appearing.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 19**: Notices Marie's reaction: "Maman, ma tante, même Walitsky et Pavloucha" all noticed she was "tellement contrariée" (so annoyed) when Lambertye didn't appear.

## Context
- Russian diminutive name (Pavel/Paul)
- Part of household or close circle
- Observant of Marie's moods
- Listed after family and Walitsky
