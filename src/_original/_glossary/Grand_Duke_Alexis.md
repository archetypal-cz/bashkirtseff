# Grand Duke Alexis

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-30

## Identity
Russian Grand Duke Alexei Alexandrovich (1850-1908), son of Emperor Alexander II. Remembered by Marie as exemplifying aristocratic perfection.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 30**: "Je me souviens vaguement des grands-ducs Voldemar et Alexis" - remembered with his brother Vladimir as men "dans le genre Hamilton," with grand and sublime bearing.

## Historical Context
- Fourth son of Alexander II
- Admiral-General of Imperial Navy
- Known for world travels
- Famous 1871-1872 American tour
- Never married

## Context
- Met in Nice or Baden-Baden
- Exemplifies aristocratic ideal
- "Hommes comme j'aime"
- Compared to Hamilton brothers
- Grand gestures and bearing
