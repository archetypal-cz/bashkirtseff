# Grand-papa

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-11

## Identity
A dance performed at social gatherings in the 1870s. Appears to be a group dance that briefly livens up parties.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 11**: At the tepid Howard dance: "à la fin quand on a dansé le grand-papa, ça a paru un peu plus gai" (at the end when they danced the grand-papa, it seemed a bit more lively).

## Context
- Social dance of the 1870s
- Group participation dance
- Used to energize flagging parties
- Named "grandfather" - possibly old-fashioned dance
- Brief excitement it provides doesn't last
