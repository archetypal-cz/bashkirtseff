# Hôtel de France

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-22

## Identity
Hotel in Nice where Papa Furstenberg stays.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 22**: Marie sees during morning ride: "A la fenêtre de l'hôtel de France, je vois papa Furstenberg fumant une pipe" (At the window of the Hôtel de France, I see Papa Furstenberg smoking a pipe).

## Context
- One of Nice's hotels for winter visitors
- Located along riding route
- Visible from street level
- Typical accommodation for wealthy visitors
