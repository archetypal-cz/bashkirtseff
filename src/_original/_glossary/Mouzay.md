# Comtesse de Mouzay

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-22

## Identity
A French countess residing in Nice who is suffering from serious illness and receives visits from Marie's family.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 22**: Marie, her mother, aunt, and Dina visit the Comtesse de Mouzay who is "fort malade" (very ill). At her home they encounter Mme Balagny.

## Context
- French aristocracy in Nice
- Seriously ill, receiving social visits despite condition
- Part of the international aristocratic network
- Her illness prompts formal sympathy visits
- Connected to other French nobles like Mme Balagny
- Represents the social duty of visiting the sick
