# La Saxe

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-17

## Identity
Courtesan in Nice, nicknamed "The Saxon" suggesting German origin.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 17**: At the theater in a ground floor box. Marie describes her as beautiful, especially her face, with a "nonchalant, ivre, languissant et gaillard" (nonchalant, drunk, languishing and lusty) air. Compares her to Phryne or Lais (famous Greek courtesans).

## Marie's Analysis
