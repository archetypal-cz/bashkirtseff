# Princess Souvoroff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-20

## Identity
Russian princess in Nice social circle. From one of Russia's most distinguished military families.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 16**: At the Détroit (Promenade des Anglais): "la princesse Souvoroff était au Détroit, elle me regarde et me suit des yeux jusqu'à ce qu'on ne peut plus voir" (Princess Souvoroff was at the Détroit, she looks at me and follows me with her eyes until one can no longer see).
- **March 20**: Visits Marie's family: "la gracieuse princesse Souvoroff" showed friendship to Maman all winter at Monte-Carlo. Leaves her portrait, forces promise to visit her new Paris house. "C'est la femme la plus aimable et la plus charmante" (She's the most amiable and charming woman).

## Context
- Suvorov family descended from Marshal Alexander Suvorov
- One of Russia's greatest military heroes (1729-1800)
- Princely title granted for military service
- Family maintains high social position
- Presence in Nice typical of Russian aristocracy
