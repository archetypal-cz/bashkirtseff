# Larcher

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-19

## Identity
Author of the book "La femme" which Marie orders from Visconti bookstore alongside Sainte-Beuve's "Les femmes célèbres."

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 19**: Marie orders "La femme" by Larcher along with Sainte-Beuve's work from Visconti.

## Context
- Contemporary author writing about women's topics
- Part of Marie's intellectual curiosity about women's roles and status
- Represents the period's growing literature on "the woman question"
- Marie's selection shows her systematic approach to understanding female experience
- Paired with Sainte-Beuve's celebrity portraits, suggests comprehensive study of women's lives
