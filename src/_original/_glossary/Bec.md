# Le Bec

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-11

## Identity
Young man who appears to be romantically interested in Marie, whom she regards with amusement.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 11**: Arrives at the end of the wedding service. Marie calls him "ce Bec amoureux!" (this lovesick Bec!).

## Context
- Appears to have romantic interest in Marie
- Marie treats his affections lightly
- Timing suggests he may have been waiting for her
- Part of Marie's social circle in Nice
