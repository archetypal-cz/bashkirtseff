**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05

## Identity
French novelist, poet, and man of letters (1815-1896). Director of the Comédie-Française and prolific author.

## Works Read by Marie

### 1874
- **"Louis XIV et ses femmes"** - About Louis XIV and his mistresses
- **"Les courtisanes du monde"** - About famous courtesans

## Marie's Response

## Historical Context
- Known for elegant, gossipy historical narratives
- Wrote extensively about French court life and famous women
- Popular with sophisticated readers interested in scandal
- His books were considered somewhat risqué for young ladies

## Significance for Marie
- Introduces her to court intrigue and sexual politics
- Feeds her fantasies about aristocratic life
- Notable that her mother permits these "scandalous" books
- Shapes her understanding of women's power through beauty/sexuality
