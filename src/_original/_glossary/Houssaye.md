**Research Status**: Basic

## Identity
French novelist and critic (1815-1896), wrote popular books about royal mistresses and courtesans.

### 1873
- Multiple references to his books on courtesans

## Literary Context
- Director of Comédie-Française
- Wrote romanticized histories of royal mistresses
- Popular with young women despite scandalous subjects
- Marie uses his work to understand aristocratic romance
