# Querteux

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-21

## Identity
Fashion house or couturier that Marie chooses alongside Laferrière as an alternative to Worth.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 21**: Marie decides to abandon Worth for "Laferrière et Querteux" because Worth is "trop massif et sévère."

## Context
- Alternative fashion house to Worth
- Paired with Laferrière in Marie's new fashion strategy
- Presumably offers a different aesthetic than Worth's heavy, severe style
- Part of the competitive Paris fashion scene
- Represents Marie's desire for lighter, more elegant clothing than Worth's imposing designs
