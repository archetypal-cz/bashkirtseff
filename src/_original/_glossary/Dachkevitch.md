# Mme Dachkevitch

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-14

## Identity
Woman in Nice/Monaco society whom Marie found sympathetic.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 14**: Marie makes her acquaintance at the Monaco casino after the pigeon shoot. Describes her as "femme sympathique" (sympathetic woman).

## Notes
- Russian surname suggests part of the Russian colony
- Positive impression unusual for Marie's generally critical assessments
- Met in the casino rooms during the social hour after shooting
