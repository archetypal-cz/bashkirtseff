# Carabacel

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-06-28
**Diary Coverage**: 1873-02-21

A hillside neighborhood in Nice, now part of the Cimiez area. In the 1870s, it was a popular area for horseback riding excursions, offering scenic views of the city and Mediterranean.

## Diary References
- **1873-02-21**: Marie goes on a horseback ride with the Howard family to Carabacel, describing it as "assez bonne promenade" (quite a good ride).

## Historical Context
Carabacel was developing as a residential area for wealthy winter visitors in the 1870s. The hillside location provided cooler temperatures and panoramic views, making it attractive for both riding and villa construction. Today it's integrated into the Cimiez district of Nice.
