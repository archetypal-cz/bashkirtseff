# Les Tutcheff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-14-15

## Identity
Russian family that Marie's mother considers as an alternative living arrangement when feeling overwhelmed by social pressures.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 14**: During the emotional breakdown after the concert humiliation, Marie's mother suggests "nous ferons mieux de vivre avec les Tutcheff" as an escape from their current difficult social position.

## Context
- Russian family, likely relatives or close family friends
- Represents a refuge from current social difficulties
- Alternative living arrangement for the Bashkirtseff family
- Symbol of retreat from Nice society when social pressures become unbearable
- Mother's suggestion indicates level of desperation about their social isolation
