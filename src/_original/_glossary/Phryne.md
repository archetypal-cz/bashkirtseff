**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05

## Historical Figure
Famous Greek courtesan (hetaera) of 4th century BCE Athens, model for Praxiteles.

## Cultural Significance
- Symbol of beauty and courtesanship in classical world
- Famous for trial where she bared her breasts to win acquittal
- Model for Aphrodite of Knidos sculpture
- Represents power through beauty in Marie's imagination
