# Mme de Bargemsat

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-14

## Identity
Part of the "troïka" of prefectural society women in Nice, along with Mmes del Borgo and d'Auzac.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 14**: At concert, Marie notes in margin: "Troïka: je nomme Mmes del Borgo, d'Auzac et de Bargemsat" - the three ladies from the prefecture sitting in front row.

## Context
- Connected to Nice prefecture (government administration)
- Part of official society
- The "troïka" nickname suggests inseparable trio
- Prominent concert seating shows social importance
- Noble particle "de" indicates aristocracy
