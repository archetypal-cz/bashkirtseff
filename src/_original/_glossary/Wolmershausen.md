**Research Status**: Basic

## Identity
London tailor at 49 Curzon Street, Mayfair.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 27**: Made the gray jacket that Galve family uses as model for new clothes at Simone's. Marie carefully notes it's "not from Manby."

- Prestigious Mayfair address
- High-end London tailoring
- Competing with other London tailors (Manby)
- Fashion imported from London to Nice
