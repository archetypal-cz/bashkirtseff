# Cannes

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-18

## Identity
Elegant resort town on the French Riviera, west of Nice, famous for its British aristocratic visitors.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 18**: Musical bands from Cannes perform at Nice exhibition/festival.

## Context
- Major Riviera resort
- British aristocracy's winter residence
- More exclusive than Nice
- Famous Croisette promenade
- Cultural rival to Nice
- Sends bands to regional events

## Historical Context
- Developed by Lord Brougham (1834)
- British colony established
- Luxury hotels and villas
- International high society
