# Karitoff

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-01

## Identity
Russian family who dines at London House and admires Marie, seeking to arrange a marriage match.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 1**: The Karitoffs (husband and wife) are "all in love" with Marie - her walk, voice, face, smile, and especially her manners. They propose matching her with a Sorokooumovski relative (son-in-law, cousin, or brother-in-law) with 400,000 roubles annual income.

## Context
- Russian merchant family
- Connected to fur trade through Sorokooumovski connection
- Part of wealthy Russian merchant class ("boutiquiers")
- Dine at fashionable London House
- Active in matchmaking among Russian expatriates
- Marie's reaction shows class consciousness despite wealth
