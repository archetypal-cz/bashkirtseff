# Simone

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-19

## Identity
Seamstress or dressmaker who handles alterations and fittings for Marie's clothing, including pieces from prestigious fashion houses like Worth.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 11**: Brings the robes that are "superbes" for the Filimonoff wedding
- **February 19**: Marie takes her new white dress from Worth to Simone for alterations
- **March 19**: Marie complains: "Simone m'ennuie plus que tout, elle me fait tellement attendre" (Simone annoys me more than anything, she makes me wait so much)

## Context
- Professional seamstress serving wealthy clientele in Nice
- Handles high-end fashion including Worth creations
- Essential part of the fashion infrastructure for international aristocracy
- Provides fitting and alteration services for designer clothing
- Trusted with expensive and delicate fabrics
