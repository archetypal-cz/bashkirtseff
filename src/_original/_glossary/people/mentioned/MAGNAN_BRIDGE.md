# Pont Magnan (Magnan Bridge)

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-01-31

## Location
Bridge in Nice over the Magnan river, marking riding territory.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **January 31**: Marie crosses it during her ride before having "a good galop" and turning right under the railway bridge.

## Context
- Part of Nice's infrastructure
- Marks boundary for safe galloping area
- Connected to railway bridge with terrible echo
- Part of Marie's regular riding routes
