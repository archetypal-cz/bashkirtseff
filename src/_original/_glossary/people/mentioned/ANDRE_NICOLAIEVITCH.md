# André Nicolaïevitch

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-16

## Identity
A good-natured older Russian gentleman, always part of the Souvoroff circle, who participates enthusiastically in Nice carnival festivities.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **February 16**: At carnival in the prefecture tribune with Princess Souvoroff and Galve. Marie calls him "un bon vieux toujours avec les Souvoroff" who "doit être très amusant." Throws many bouquets at their carriage along with M. de Galve.

## Context
- Russian name suggesting noble background
- Permanent member of Souvoroff social circle  
- Described as amusing and good-natured
- Active participant in carnival festivities
- Represents the kind older generation within aristocratic society
- Marie appreciates his playful spirit during public celebrations
