# Sorokooumovski

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-01

## Identity
Wealthy Russian fur merchant family with a bachelor heir proposed as match for Marie.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 1**: Proposed as "un fiancé excellent" (excellent fiancé) for Marie by the Karitoffs. Has 400,000 roubles annual income. Related to Karitoffs as either son-in-law, cousin, or brother-in-law. Marie horrified by the name: "So-ro-ko-ou-mov-ski!"

## Context
- Fur trade merchants ("vendent des fourrures")
- Extremely wealthy ("richissimes")
- 400,000 roubles annual income (enormous sum)
- Marie calls them "braves boutiquiers" (good shopkeepers) - condescending
- Name analysis: Сорокоумовский (forty-minded/clever)
- Represents new merchant wealth vs old nobility
