# Margot

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-01-24
**Diary Coverage**: Up to July 1881

Margot is the three-year-old daughter of Sperandio and his wife (Pacha's sister). Marie describes her as "gentille" (sweet/nice).

**Key Diary References**:
- July 15, 1881: "Sperandio vient avec sa femme (sœur de Pacha) qui est la femme la plus laide que j'aie jamais vue peut-être, et sa petite fille Margot, trois ans et gentille"

**Context**:
- Daughter of Sperandio and his wife
- Three years old in July 1881
- Niece of Pacha
- Part of the extended household/family circle at Gavronzi
- Travels with family to Karkoff

**Research Notes**:
- Full name likely Margarita (Margot is diminutive)
- Born approximately 1878
- Marie's only positive comment about Sperandio's immediate family (she found his wife very ugly)
