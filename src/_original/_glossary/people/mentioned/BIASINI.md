# Biasini

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-29

## Identity
Peasant who visits the Bashkirtseff household and causes scandal by being given a cigarette in their salon.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 29**: His visit leads to "débats furieux et des scènes déchirantes" (furious debates and heart-rending scenes). Marie is outraged when "On avait donné une cigarette à ce paysan, dans notre salon, devant moi !" causing her to weep and withdraw.

## Context
- Italian surname suggests local peasant
- Class boundary violation
- Smoking in salon inappropriate
- Marie's class consciousness triggered
- Causes family conflict
