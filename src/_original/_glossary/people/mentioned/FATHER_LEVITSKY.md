# Father Levitsky

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-05

## Identity
Russian Orthodox priest who will perform the blessing ceremony for the Bashkirtseff's new villa.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 5**: Marie invites people to attend the "baptême de notre villa, par le père Levitsky" (baptism of our villa by Father Levitsky) scheduled for the next day at 2 PM.

## Context
- Russian Orthodox clergy serving the émigré community in Nice
- House blessings are traditional in Orthodox practice
- The ceremony marks the formal taking of possession
- Marie uses "baptême" (baptism) metaphorically for house blessing
- Shows the family maintaining Orthodox religious traditions

## Cultural Notes
- Orthodox house blessings involve prayers and holy water
- Traditional ceremony when moving to new home
- Important social event for Russian community
