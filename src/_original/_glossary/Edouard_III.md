**Research Status**: Basic

## Identity
King Edward III of England (1312-1377), referenced in connection with Black Prince.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 22**: Marie crowns her horse Black Prince with roses and jokes: "Si Edouard III le voyait !!!" (If Edward III saw this!!!).

## Context
- Historical reference showing Marie's education
- Father of the Black Prince (Edward of Woodstock)
- Marie's horse named after his son
- Humorous contrast: war horse vs. pleasure riding
