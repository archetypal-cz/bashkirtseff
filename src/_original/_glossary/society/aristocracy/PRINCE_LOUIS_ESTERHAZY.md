# Prince Louis Esterhazy

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-04-23

## Identity
Hungarian prince, renowned gentleman-rider who stayed at Shepheard's Hotel with Hamilton and won races on Hamilton's horse.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **April 23**: Quoted from Le Derby: stayed at Shepheard's Hotel "en même temps que ces messieurs" (Hamilton and Mandeville). Known at Baden, has "la réputation d'être l'un des premiers gentlemen-riders de son époque" (reputation as one of the premier gentleman-riders of his era). Won several important steeplechases on Hamilton's horse Bar-le-Duc. Departing for Vienna.
