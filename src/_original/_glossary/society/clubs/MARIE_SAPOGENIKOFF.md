# Marie Sapogenikoff

**Research Status**: Moderate
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-10
**Diary Coverage**: Up to July 1, 1875

One of the Sapogenikoff daughters, a close friend of Marie Bashkirtseff in Nice during 1875. She is part of the Russian expatriate community and participates in social adventures with Marie.

## Character Description

Marie Sapogenikoff is adventurous and willing to participate in Marie Bashkirtseff's schemes. She has a landau (carriage) and often provides transportation for their group expeditions.

## Key Events

- **May 15, 1875**: Takes Marie Bashkirtseff to see Audiffret's château in her landau
- **Three Graces Society**: Takes the name "Aglaé" (one of the three Graces from Greek mythology)

## Social Role

Part of the "Three Graces" society formed by the young ladies:
- Marie Bashkirtseff = Thalie (Muse of comedy)
- Marie Sapogenikoff = Aglaé (Grace of elegance)
- Olga Sapogenikoff = Euphrosine (Grace of joy)

## Related People

- Olga Sapogenikoff (sister)
- Marie Bashkirtseff (close friend)
- Audiffret (object of their interest)

## Research Notes

Represents the type of adventurous young Russian women in Nice's expatriate society. Her willingness to participate in Marie's schemes shows the close bonds formed within this community.
