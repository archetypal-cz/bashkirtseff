# Sabatier Family

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-06
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-03-15

## Identity
Wealthy French family with at least two marriageable daughters in Nice society.

## Diary References

### 1874
- **March 15**: Elder daughter marrying M. Bernisse. Younger daughter marrying John de Lewin with 2 million franc dowry: "Il épouse Mlle Sabatier la cadette, qui lui apporte deux millions de dot."

## Context
- Very wealthy family (2 million franc dowry)
- Two daughters: "l'aînée" (elder) and "la cadette" (younger)
- Both marrying in 1874
- Younger daughter's marriage to confirmed bachelor Lewin surprises everyone
- The enormous dowry suggests major fortune
