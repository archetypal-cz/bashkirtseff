# M. Berr

**Research Status**: Basic
**Last Updated**: 2025-07-05
**Diary Coverage**: Up to 1874-02-11

## Identity

- **February 11**: One of the "garçons d'honneur" (groomsmen) with Woerman and Bashkirtseff. "Berr tenait ferme" holding the crown during ceremony. Marie calls him "un jeune homme très bien" but fears he's interested in the second Filimonoff daughter.
